Stephen Hawking

"A man who illuminated the darkest corners of the universe and beyond."

Here's a time line of Stephen Hawking's life:

  1. 1942 Born on January 8th in Oxford, England.
  2. 1959 Enrolls at the University of Oxford.
  3. 1962 Graduates with a degree in Natural Science from the University of Oxford.
  4. 1963 Diagnosed with amyotrophic lateral sclerosis (ALS).
  5. 1965 Marries Jane Wilde.
  6. 1970 Earns his Ph.D. in Cosmology.
  7. 1973 Publishes his theory on Hawking radiation.
  8. 1979 Publishes his book "A Brief History of Time".
  9. 1985 Marries Elaine Mason.
  10. 2001 Publishes "The Universe in a Nutshell".
  11. 2007 Experiences weightlessness on a zero-gravity flight.
  12. 2014 Publishes his autobiography "My Brief History".
  13. 2018 Passes away on March 14th in Cambridge, England.
  14. 2019 "The Theory of Everything" documentary based on his life is released.
  15. 2020 Announcement of the launch of the Stephen Hawking Satellite in his honor.
